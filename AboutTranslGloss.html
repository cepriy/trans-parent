This is an ideographic terminological dictionary of translation studies terminology.
Lack of encyclopedic definitions for many translation terms in Ukrainian requires both contextual and semantically relational approach for terms explanations. Thus, this dictionary allows to observe translation term in their context and in relation to other terms of the same semantic field.
This data can be use to mine the terms' meaning, unveil tiny differences among some close terms. To search for definitions and othe relevant characteristics of a term press "Terminological entry". To compile a thesaurus entere a term or its part and press "Thesaurus".
